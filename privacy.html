Privacy Policy — Log my Lifts

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

Log my Lifts stores all workout data locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit personal data to any server.

Apple HealthKit

This app uses Apple HealthKit to read body weight, body fat percentage, and heart rate data. This information is used solely to display personal fitness metrics within the app. HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, never uploaded to any server, and never used for advertising.

iCloud

If you enable iCloud, your workout data is synced via Apple's iCloud infrastructure under Apple's own privacy policy.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We do not store payment information.
