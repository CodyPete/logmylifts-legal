Privacy Policy — PTClientTracker

Last updated: May 2026

PTClientTracker is a personal trainer client management app for iOS. This policy explains what data is handled and how.

Data Stored Locally

All client profiles, workout prescriptions, session logs, and assessment records are stored locally on your device using iOS file storage. No data is transmitted to any PTClientTracker server.

Oura Ring Integration

If a client connects their Oura account, PTClientTracker uses the Oura API to read daily readiness scores, sleep data, heart rate, and related recovery metrics. This data is retrieved directly from Oura's servers and displayed within the app. It is not stored permanently or shared with any third party beyond the device. Oura's own Privacy Policy applies to data held by Oura.

Strava Integration

If a client connects their Strava account, PTClientTracker uses the Strava API to read and optionally create activity records. Data retrieved from Strava is used solely to display workout information within the app. Strava's own Privacy Policy applies to data held by Strava.

Apple HealthKit

The app may read body weight and heart rate from Apple HealthKit where available. HealthKit data is used for display purposes only and is never uploaded to any server or shared with third parties.

No Analytics or Advertising

PTClientTracker does not use any analytics SDK, advertising network, or crash-reporting service that transmits personal data off-device.

Data Deletion

All locally stored data can be removed by deleting the app from your device. To revoke Oura or Strava access, disconnect the integration from within the app or revoke access in your Oura / Strava account settings.
